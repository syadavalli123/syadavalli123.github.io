About

Shown below is an overview of our product.

  • 2002, December 28

    The Birth of LinkedIn - Mountain View, CA

    Founded by Reid Hoffman (Venture Capitalist), Allen Blue (Product Designer), Konstantin Guericke (Marketing Professional), Eric Ly (Engineer), and Jean-Luc Vaillant (Engineer).

  • May 2003

    LinkedIn becomes launched

    Became officially launched by the founders.

  • June 2009

    Rise of a new CEO

    Jeff Weiner becomes appointed as the CEO of LinkedIn.

  • 2013

    Growth and Expansion

    Reaches 225 million members and grows at a milestone rate.

  • 2016

    Joining forces with Microsoft

    Gets acquired by Microsoft and both collaborate to stimulate professional growth across the globe.

  • Together, we shall make a huge impact!
